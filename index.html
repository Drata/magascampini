Who I am

Hey there! My name is Maga Scampini, I'm 20 years old and I'm a model and actress, that loves artistic expression in all types and forms. I also have other artistic hobbies like drawing, makeup and singing.

My Story

I was born in Venezuela. I've been in love with theater since I can remember but my experience with it started when I was 13 in my school basement. I started in a lovely small group where our plays were mostly family friendly and kid friendly, but a lot of great Venezuelan actors have come out from there to a more professional environment, I was ready to start my career as an actress when I graduated high school but my life took a turn and I had to move out of my country and started my new life in Madrid, Spain. I had to stop my dream for a while and work as a waitress to be able to pay for my studies, in the meantime I still was taking little impro classes. In 2019 I finally could get back to my passion and I started studying visual arts and dance with a physical theater of movement mention, I'm currently studying and getting more professional everyday but I wanna start to get my career going right now!
